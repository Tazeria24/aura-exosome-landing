## 2. Marketing & Profit Margin Scenarios

Since AURA EXOSOME™ is a premium, high-margin product (82%+ Contribution Margin), you have exceptional room to invest in Customer Acquisition Costs (CAC) via TikTok and Instagram ads.

### Scenario A: Conservative / Launch Phase (High CAC)
*Suitable for the first 3 months as you build brand authority and run pixel optimizations.*
*   **Target CAC:** **$25.00** (Acquiring one customer costs $25.00 in ad spend)
*   **Net Profit per Unit:** **$27.53**
*   **Net Margin:** **43.02%**
*   *Verdict: Extremely healthy. Even with high early-stage ad costs, you yield over 40% net margins.*

### Scenario B: Optimized / Scale Phase (Target CAC)
*Achieved once your retargeting, email flows, and social proof are mature.*
*   **Target CAC:** **$18.00**
*   **Net Profit per Unit:** **$34.53**
*   **Net Margin:** **53.95%**
*   *Verdict: Industry elite. Over 50% net profit is achievable due to the low formulation COGS of biotech cosmetics.*

### Scenario C: Organic / LTV Phase (Repeat Customers)
*Applicable to subscription orders, email newsletter flows, and word-of-mouth repeat buyers.*
*   **Target CAC:** **$0.00** (Zero marketing cost)
*   **Net Profit per Unit:** **$52.53**
*   **Net Margin:** **82.08%**
*   *Verdict: This is where true e-commerce wealth is built. Retaining 30% of your buyers on a monthly auto-ship subscription will maximize LTV.*

## 3. Financial Breakeven Analysis

To cover standard monthly operating overhead (e.g., $1,500/month for advanced ads, Shopify plan, design tools, and software integrations):

*   **Fixed Monthly Overhead:** $1,500.00
*   **Contribution Margin per Unit:** $52.53
*   **Monthly Breakeven Units:** **29 Bottles / month**
*   **Daily Sales Volume Target:** **1 Bottle / day**

With just one sale per day, your brand is entirely self-sustaining. Everything beyond 29 units per month is pure net profit.
